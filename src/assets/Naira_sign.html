Got questions?

Here are answers to the most commonly asked questions about Etax.

What is my Tax-Payer ID?

Tax-Payer ID is a unique number attributed to a registered individual/corporate body on the etax system. This allows users to manage their tax accounts and pay taxes to LIRS nationwide.

Can I use both my BVN and NIN to register?

No you can not. You can only use one identification type to register on etax.

How can I pay my taxes using ETAX?

As a registered taxpayer, make instant tax payments and download your receipts immediately using the secure e-payment channels.

What do I do if I forget my taxpayer ID?

Click on the Forgot Taxpayer ID link and provide required information. Your unique Taxpayer ID would be sent to your registered email address and phone number.
